========================================
       MINECRAFT SURVIVAL CLI
========================================

Welcome to Minecraft Survival CLI! Your ultimate goal is to survive, gear up, and defeat the final boss: The Ender Dragon.

--- GAME MECHANICS ---
1. STATS:
- Health (100 Max): If it hits 0, you die and the game is over.
- Hunger (100 Max): Decreases by 1 every time you perform an action. If it reaches 0, you will starve and lose 5 Health per turn! Eat Food to restore Hunger.
- XP & Level: Gained by doing activities. Leveling up shows your progression.

2. GATHERING RESOURCES:
- Wood & Food can be gathered without tools.
- To mine ores, you need the right Pickaxe:
  * Stone/Coal: Needs Wooden Pickaxe
  * Iron: Needs Stone Pickaxe
  * Gold: Needs Iron Pickaxe
  * Diamond: Needs Diamond Pickaxe

3. CRAFTING:
- Upgrade your tools! A better Pickaxe lets you mine rarer ores, and a better Sword lets you fight stronger enemies.
- Craft a Shield, Diamond Armor, and a Wooden Torch for extra progression.

4. FIGHTING:
- You must have the appropriate Sword level to fight tougher enemies.
- Enemies:
  * Zombie (Easy) -> Needs Wooden Sword
  * Skeleton (Normal) -> Needs Stone Sword
  * Creeper (Hard) -> Needs Iron Sword
  * Enderman (Very Hard) -> Needs Iron Sword
  * Dragon (Final Boss) -> Needs Diamond Sword & Health > 70
- Defeating enemies grants XP.

5. BUILDING:
- Build structures to upgrade your base.
- Wood House -> Stone House -> Iron House -> Castle.
- Farm: Produces Food overnight while you sleep.
- Furnace: Can be built with Stone.

6. SLEEPING & EVENTS:
- Sleep to advance to the next day and restore 20 Health.
- Beware of random events during the night:
  * Day 5 (and multiples): Monster Attacks (You need a sword to defend).
  * Day 7 (and multiples): Rain (Boosts Farm output).
  * Day 10 (and multiples): Find a Treasure Chest (+50 XP).
  * Day 13 (and multiples): Earthquake (Damages your house).

--- HOW TO WIN ---
Craft a Diamond Sword, ensure your Health is above 70, and select the "Fight" -> "Dragon" option. Defeating the Ender Dragon instantly wins you the game!

Good luck, and try to unlock all the achievements!
